Survive

Gather resources, craft tools, place blocks, hunt animals, fight enemies, and survive the night.

Move with WASD or arrow keys. E gathers. Space attacks. F eats food or meat. Select Wall or Torch, then press Place Selected to place it in front of your character.

Walls cost 5 wood. Torches cost 4 wood and 2 stone. Torches can be picked up and moved. Animals drop meat. Enemies drop resources when defeated.
